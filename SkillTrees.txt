Music Theory (Usage of Sheet Music/magic)
-Set Length: Increase how much Sheet Music you can have prepped at once
-Subgenre: Extra elemental damage buffs depending on genre
-Power Chord: Decreases cooldowns

Performance (Movement and stuff that takes stamina)
-Crowd Surf: Can jump on enemy heads
-Green Room: Can heal in safe zones
-Countermelody: Parry

Mosh (Physical attacking and whatnot)
-Headbang: Increases physical damage
-In the Pit: Hitting more than 1 enemy at once deals extra damage
-Throwing Elbows: Thorns damage
